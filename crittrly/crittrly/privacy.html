Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Crittrly ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What We Collect

When you place an order, we collect: your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information. Payment card data is processed securely via Stripe and never stored on our servers.

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to: fulfill your orders, send order confirmations and tracking updates, respond to customer support requests, and send marketing emails (only if you opt in).

Fulfillment Partners

Your shipping address and order details are shared with our fulfillment partners solely for the purpose of delivering your order. These partners act as data processors under our instruction.

Cookies

We use browser localStorage to save your cart and wishlist. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing hello@crittrly.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Contact

Questions? Email us at hello@crittrly.com.
